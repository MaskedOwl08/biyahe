Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Biyaheng Pantalan ("the App," "we," "our") handles information when you use our mobile application and website.

1. Information We Collect

Biyaheng Pantalan is a ferry schedule board app for Cuyo, Palawan. The App displays publicly available ferry schedule information and does not require account creation, login, or the submission of any personal information (such as your name, email address, or location) to use its core features.

2. Advertising

The App does not currently display advertisements. We may introduce advertising through third-party advertising networks (such as Google AdMob) in a future update. If we do, this Privacy Policy will be updated in advance to describe what information those advertising partners collect (which can include device identifiers, general device information, approximate location, and ad interaction data) and how you can manage your ad preferences.

You can review Google's advertising practices here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

3. Data We Store

Ferry schedule content shown in the App is stored and served via Firebase Firestore (Google Cloud). This data consists of publicly available schedule information and is not linked to individual users.

4. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since no personal data is collected for the App's core functionality, it is generally safe for all audiences; however, the advertising network's own policies regarding children's data apply as described in Section 2.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal information with third parties, aside from the advertising network described above, which operates independently under its own privacy policy.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

7. Contact Us
